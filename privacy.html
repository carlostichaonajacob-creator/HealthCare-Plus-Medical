2 Information We Collect

We collect various types of information necessary to provide you with quality medical care and services:

Personal Information

  • Identification Details: Full name, date of birth, ID number, gender, contact details (address, phone, email)
  • Demographic Information: Language preference, marital status, occupation (when relevant to care)
  • Emergency Contact: Names and contact details of next of kin or emergency contacts

  • Medical History: Past and current medical conditions, allergies, medications, immunization records
  • Clinical Information: Symptoms, diagnoses, treatment plans, test results, clinical notes
  • Prescriptions: Medication records and pharmacy information
  • Lifestyle Information: Relevant lifestyle factors affecting your health

3 How We Use Your Information

We use your personal and medical information for the following purposes:

Medical Care & Treatment

  • Providing medical consultations, examinations, and treatments
  • Maintaining accurate medical records
  • Coordinating care with specialists, laboratories, and other healthcare providers
  • Prescribing medications and monitoring your health
  • Following up on your treatment and progress

Administrative Purposes

  • Scheduling and managing appointments
  • Sending appointment reminders via SMS or email
  • Billing and payment processing
  • Processing insurance claims
  • Maintaining practice records and statistics

Legal & Regulatory Compliance

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Responding to legal requests and court orders
  • Reporting to regulatory bodies when required
Your Consent

We will always obtain your explicit consent before using your information for purposes not directly related to your care, such as research or marketing communications.

4 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We take your privacy seriously and only share your information when necessary and lawful. Your personal and medical information may be shared with:

Healthcare Providers

  • Specialists: When we refer you for specialized care
  • Hospitals: For emergency care or hospital admissions
  • Laboratories: For diagnostic tests and results
  • Pharmacies: For prescription fulfillment
  • Other practitioners: When involved in your coordinated care

Service Providers

  • IT Service Providers: For secure data storage and management
  • Billing Companies: For payment processing
  • Communication Services: For appointment reminders

Legal Requirements

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Law enforcement requests
  • Public health reporting (infectious diseases, etc.)
  • Prevention of serious harm or criminal activity
No Unauthorized Sharing

We will NEVER sell, rent, or trade your personal or medical information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely for your care and the administrative purposes outlined in this policy.

5 Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal and medical information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction:

Technical Security

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Firewalls: Network security to prevent unauthorized access
  • Access Controls: Role-based access permissions
  • Secure Passwords: Strong password policies for all systems
  • Regular Updates: Software and security patches kept current
  • Backups: Regular encrypted backups stored securely

Administrative Security

  • Staff Training: All staff trained on data protection
  • Confidentiality Agreements: All staff sign confidentiality agreements
  • Access Logs: Monitoring and auditing of data access
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments

Data Breach Protocol

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  1. Immediately investigate and contain the breach
  2. Notify relevant authorities within required timeframes
  3. Inform affected individuals without undue delay
  4. Take corrective actions to prevent future incidents
Your Role in Security

You can help protect your information by keeping your contact details updated, not sharing login credentials (if applicable), and reporting any suspicious activity to us immediately.

6 Your Privacy Rights

You have important rights regarding your personal information. We are committed to respecting and facilitating these rights:

Right to Access

You have the right to request access to your personal and medical information that we hold.

Right to Correction

You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Deletion

You may request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Object

You can object to certain processing of your information, such as marketing communications.

Right to Portability

You can request a copy of your medical records in a commonly used format.

Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time (where consent is the legal basis).

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Submit a written request to: info@healthcareplus.com
  • Visit our practice in person with valid identification
  • Call us at +1 (555) 123-4567

We will respond to your request within 30 days. In some cases, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Limitations

Some rights may be limited by legal obligations. For example:

  • We must retain medical records for the period required by law
  • We cannot delete information needed for ongoing legal claims
  • We may be legally required to retain certain data for regulatory purposes
