3 Appointments & Cancellations

3.1 Booking Appointments

Appointments can be made by:

  • Calling our practice during business hours
  • Using our online booking system (if available)
  • Visiting our practice in person

3.2 Cancellation Policy

We understand that circumstances change. However, to ensure we can serve all our patients effectively:

  • 24-Hour Notice: Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations
  • Late Cancellations: Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may incur a fee
  • No-Shows: Failure to attend without notice may result in a missed appointment fee
  • Repeated No-Shows: Patients who repeatedly miss appointments may be asked to prepay for future bookings

3.3 Late Arrivals

If you arrive more than 15 minutes late for your appointment, we may need to reschedule to avoid delays for other patients. Please contact us if you are running late.

Appointment Duration

Standard consultations are scheduled for 15-20 minutes. If you require a longer appointment for multiple concerns, please request an extended consultation when booking.
